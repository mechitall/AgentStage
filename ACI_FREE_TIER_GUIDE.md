## 🚀 **5-Minute Setup Guide**

### **Step 1: Create Your Free Account**
1. Visit [platform.aci.dev](https://platform.aci.dev/)
2. Sign up with your email (completely free!)
3. Verify your account

### **Step 2: Create a Project & Agent**
1. Click "Create Project" (free unlimited projects)
2. Name it "Agent Stage Political Simulation"
3. Create your first agent (also free!)

### **Step 3: Configure Free Tier Apps**
Available on FREE TIER:
- ✅ **BRAVE_SEARCH** - Web search with generous daily limits
- ✅ **GITHUB** - Repository search and analysis
- ✅ **ARXIV** - Academic paper search
- ✅ **WEATHER** - Environmental data (if needed)

**To Configure:**
1. Go to "App Store" in your dashboard
2. Find and configure "BRAVE_SEARCH"
3. Find and configure "GITHUB"
4. Complete OAuth flows (free!)

### **Step 4: Link Your Accounts**
1. Navigate to "App Configurations"
2. Click "Add Account" for each app
3. Complete the free OAuth flows
4. Choose a unique **Linked Account Owner ID** (e.g., your email)

### **Step 5: Get Your Credentials**
1. Go to your Agent settings
2. Copy your **API Key**
3. Note your **Linked Account Owner ID**
4. Add both to your `.env` file
